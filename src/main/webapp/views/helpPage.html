Recruitment Portal

Roles Available
Role_Admin - (Dashboard, Recruitment, Referral, Admin)
Role_HR - (Dashboard, Recruitment, Referral)
Role_Manager - (Dashboard, Recruitment, Referral)
Role_Requsition_Manager - (Dashboard, Recruitment, Referral)
Role_Interviewer - (Dashboard, Recruitment, Referral) Sub tab only interview
Role_Requisition_Approver - (Dashboard, Recruitment, Referral)
Role_User - (Dashboard, Referral)
Features
Dashboard
This page will provide you an overview of No. of Positions , Interview Schedule , Profiles Referred based on the role of User.
Profile
We can Add , View and Update candidate profiles, also can see the source ie Referral or Recruiter.
Requisition
We can create a Requisition as per the need of Organization , and assign it to the Resource Managers for the approval. Approval will be done at two Levels , Out of which 1 is mandatory, also We have provided Reject Functionality in case requisition requirement has been changed or no more needed . Approved / Reject button will be visible to assigned approver only.
Position
We can View & Update all the positions created after requisition is approved, also we can see Profiles which are assigned for specific Position and their current status. Based on Profile's status , we can update the status of Position as well.
Interview
We can schedule the interview and Submit the feedback for Profiles. Also we have added a functionality to assign multiple rounds of Interview without submitting the feedback of previous round.
Referral
We can see all referral positions under position tab , and referred Profiles by logged in User under Profile Tab.
Admin
We can Add/Update all the static data of Portal like , User Roles, Interview Rounds, Client Specific Pre-defined Job Descriptions. This tab will only be visible to ROLE_ADMIN.
